Frequently Asked Questions

How does the credit system work?

Our credit system is simple: for every 100 bytes of text you analyze, you use 1 credit. Each review is typically 1,000 bytes, so a typical analysis of 30 reviews would consume approximately 300 credits.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or purchase additional credits at any time. Your existing credits will be added to your new ones. Downgrades will take effect upon your next billing cycle.

Do my credits expire?

No, your credits don't expire as long as your account remains active. You can use them whenever you need, with no time limit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. All transactions are processed securely through our payment provider.

Can I get a refund?

We don't offer refunds for credits that have been purchased. However, if you experience any technical issues that prevent you from using our service, please contact our support team.

Do you offer custom enterprise plans?

Yes, we offer custom enterprise solutions for teams with higher volume needs. Please contact our sales team at enterprise@intrix.com to discuss your requirements.

Ready to start analyzing emotions?

Begin with our Starter package and upgrade anytime as your needs grow. No long-term commitments required.
